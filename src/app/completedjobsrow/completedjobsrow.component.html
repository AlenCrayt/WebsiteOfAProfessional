Issues with overheating? No problem!

This PC suffered from sporadic shutdowns and erratic behavior. Upon thorough examination, it became apparent that the root cause lay in a faulty power supply unit (PSU) and overheating components. I replaced the failing PSU with a high-quality unit, ensuring stable and reliable power delivery to the system. Additionally, I meticulously cleaned accumulated dust from the CPU heatsink and case fans, restoring proper airflow and mitigating overheating issues. With precision and care, the hardware woes were swiftly addressed, culminating in a rejuvenated PC.
